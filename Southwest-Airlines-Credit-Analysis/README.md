# Southwest Airlines Co. (NYSE: LUV) — Independent Credit Analysis

**Date:** June 2026

**Filing Used:** FY2025 Form 10-K, Q1 2026 Form 10-Q (SEC EDGAR)
**Tools:** Excel (3-tab financial model), credit memo (Word)
**Recommendation:** Approve with Conditions

## Methodology
- Extracted financials directly from SEC EDGAR filings
- Calculated 6 key credit ratios across 3 years (2023–2025) with benchmark flagging
- Identified and adjusted for Air Traffic Liability distortion in the current ratio
- Built a 3-scenario stress test (Base, Adverse, Severely Adverse) projecting Debt/EBITDA and interest coverage through 2028
- Produced a structured credit memo with strengths, risks, and a covenant-based recommendation

## Key Findings
- Debt/EBITDA improved from 4.57x (2023) to 2.30x (2025), now below the airline industry average
- EBITDA/Interest nearly doubled from 6.7x to 11.9x over the same period
- Adjusted current ratio (excluding Air Traffic Liability) is healthy at 1.13x, despite a concerning headline figure of 0.52x
- Primary risk identified: structural operational fragility tied to the point-to-point network model, evidenced by the December 2022 meltdown
